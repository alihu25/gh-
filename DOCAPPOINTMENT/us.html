About the Creator

This project, Heal+, was created by a dedicated student with the vision of simplifying the healthcare process through technology. The student’s goal was to design a modern, user-friendly platform that could bridge the gap between patients and doctors.

This project was not just an individual effort but a culmination of the support and guidance provided by our teacher, Ms. Muna. Her expertise, encouragement, and commitment to nurturing our learning journey have been instrumental in bringing this project to life.

The student hopes that this project can inspire others to think creatively, solve real-world problems, and embrace the endless possibilities of web development. Heal+ is dedicated to everyone as a showcase of what collaboration, effort, and mentorship can achieve.

A special thank you to Ms. Muna for believing in her students and pushing them to reach their full potential.
